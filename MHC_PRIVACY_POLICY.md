## Habit Checklist: Privacy policy

Habit Checklist is a simple habit tracking application developed by Masfran and available on Google Play.

### Data Collection and Use

Habit Checklist does not collect, store, share, or process any personal or sensitive user data. All habits and checklist data created by the user are stored locally on the user’s device only and are never transmitted to any external servers.

### Third-Party Services

Habit Checklist does not use any third-party services that collect user data.

### Children’s Privacy

Habit Checklist does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Since no data is collected, the app is safe for users of all ages.

### Security

Because no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of external data exposure. All user data remains on the user’s device.

### Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
